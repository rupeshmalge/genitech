Process

Built the same way, no matter the sector.

Different markets, same discipline. Here's what happens after you get in touch — pick the side that applies to you.

01

Brief

A sector-specialist consultant scopes the role, the team, and what's actually made past hires succeed or fail.

02

Search

We work our sector network first — most shortlists include people not actively job-hunting.

03

Shortlist

You receive 3–5 screened candidates within 6 working days on average, with notes on fit and motivation.

04

Interview

We coordinate scheduling, gather feedback fast, and manage counter-offer conversations before they derail things.

05

Aftercare

We check in at 30, 60, and 90 days. If it's not working out inside the guarantee period, we requalify at no extra fee.

01

Register

Tell us your sector, your experience, and what would actually make you move.

02

Match

Your consultant only puts you forward for roles that fit — not a volume list of loosely related openings.

03

Prepare

You get a real briefing on the team, the interviewer, and what they're likely to probe on.

04

Offer

We handle the back-and-forth on package and start date so you're not negotiating alone.

05

Settle in

We follow up through your first three months to make sure the role matches what was promised.
